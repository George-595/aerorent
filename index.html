Bad Weather Policy

We understand that the British weather can be unpredictable. If you are unable to fly due to adverse weather conditions (e.g., high winds or heavy rain), you can cancel your rental for a refund. We will refund the full rental cost, minus a fee to cover two-way postage (£8.99 each way, for a total of £17.98). To be eligible, you must notify us before 10 AM on the first day of your rental period.
